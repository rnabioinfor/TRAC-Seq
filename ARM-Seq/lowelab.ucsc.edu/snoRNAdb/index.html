Lowe Lab C/D box snoRNA Database

C/D box snoRNAs

We have developed an algorithm for searching genomic sequence for 2'-O-ribose methylation guide snoRNA genes. Here we provide our predictions, many of which have been experimentally verified. We also include information on previously identified methylation guide snoRNAs. Please don't hesitate to contact us (see below) if you have comments or notice errors in our annotation or attribution of previous snoRNA work.
